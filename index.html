About Us

Vivekananda College of Arts, Science and Commerce (Autonomous), Puttur: With immense pleasure and pride we present ourselves as the Premier Institution of Vivekananda Vidyavardhaka Sangha (V.V.S.) formerly known as Puttur Education Society (P.E.S.) which has almost a century’s history having been established in 1915 itself. Vivekananda College of Arts, Science and Commerce – recently elevated to the status of Autonomous College, presents itself as an educational institution striving for excellence with holistic approach. Vivekananda College was established in 1965 and celebrated its Golden Jubilee in 2015. These 58 years of fruitful existence has been a saga of planning and implementing; building and rebuilding; growth and progress; introspection and celebration.
